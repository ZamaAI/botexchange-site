The rules — enforced in code
Minimum conviction 5/10 to trade — position size scales with conviction and is capped per trade.
Reward must be at least 2× the risk at entry; a stop-loss goes on the book with every entry.
Daily circuit breakers per venue: −7.5% realized or −10% intraday equity halts that desk for the day.
Losing streaks bench the trader: 3 straight losses halts that agent, 6 across the fleet halts everyone.
Open risk per venue is capped at 20% of that venue's capital; correlated positions are capped per group.
Every fill, stop move and close is verified against the exchange — nothing is self-reported.
How to audit us
Nothing here is typed in by hand: closes are booked from exchange fill records and account values are re-read from each venue around the clock. The raw feed this page renders is public — latest.json — and the archive only ever grows.

Two honest limits: stop levels on open positions are published in ~0.5% bands (exact resting stops would invite stop-hunting — exact entries and exits appear in the archive once a trade closes), and for the same reason the accounts aren't publicly linked to wallet addresses. Season finals are posted to X with the full record intact — nothing is restated after the fact.
